# Research Design Guide

## Research Question Development

### FINER Criteria

* **F**easible
* **I**nteresting
* **N**ovel
* **E**thical
* **R**elevant

### Refinement

Start broad -> Narrow progressively.

* *Draft 1*: "Social media and politics."
* *Final*: "Does increased exposure to ideologically homogeneous Twitter feeds increase affective polarization among politically engaged US adults?"

## Literature Review Strategy

### Systematic Search

1. **Identify concepts**.
2. **Generate synonyms**.
3. **Combine with Booleans** (AND, OR).
4. **Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria** (Date, Language, Type).

### Database Selection

* **Multidisciplinary**: Google Scholar, Scopus.
* **Health**: PubMed.
* **Social Sciences**: JSTOR.
* **CS/Engineering**: IEEE Xplore.

## Research Ethics

### Checklist

* [ ] IRB approval / Ethical review.
* [ ] Informed consent.
* [ ] Data privacy/security.
* [ ] Authorship agreement.
* [ ] Conflict of interest disclosure.

### Misconduct to Avoid

* **Fabrication**: Making up data.
* **Falsification**: Manipulating data.
* **Plagiarism**: Using others' words without attribution.
* **P-hacking**: Fishing for significance.
